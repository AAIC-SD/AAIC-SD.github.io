Radon Testing

Protect Your Family from Radon

Why Test for Radon?

Radon is a naturally occurring radioactive gas that seeps into homes through the foundation. It's the #1 cause of lung cancer among non-smokers. The Black Hills region has some of the highest radon levels in the country.

We use professional continuous radon monitors to accurately measure levels over a 48-hour testing period.

Understanding Your Results

Elevated — Consider Mitigation
